# Phishing Detection and Awareness Report

This project focuses on phishing detection, email analysis, and user awareness. The purpose of this project is to analyse phishing indicators found in suspicious emails and explain how phishing attacks are initiated, identified, classified, and prevented.

The report discusses common phishing techniques used by attackers to trick users into revealing sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, OTPs, and banking information. It also explains how organisations and individuals can reduce the risk of phishing attacks through awareness and security best practices.

## Objectives

- Understand how phishing attacks are initiated
- Identify common phishing indicators
- Classify emails based on risk level
- Analyse suspicious email behaviour
- Explain prevention and mitigation methods
- Improve phishing awareness and security knowledge

## Tools Used

- Google Email Header Analyzer
- Browser-based phishing analysis tools
- Manual inspection of email headers and domains

## Analysis Approach

The analysis process involved examining suspicious emails and identifying indicators commonly associated with phishing attacks. Email headers were analysed using Google's Email Header Analyzer to inspect authentication results and verify whether emails originated from trusted mail servers.

The following elements were analysed during the investigation:

- Sender address and sending domain
- Email authentication results
- Subject lines designed to create urgency
- Suspicious links and redirections
- Malicious or unexpected attachments
- Grammar and spelling inconsistencies
- Context and legitimacy of the email

Emails were then classified into three categories:

- Safe
- Suspicious
- Phishing

The project also focused on understanding how attackers use social engineering techniques to manipulate users into clicking malicious links or revealing confidential information.

## Topics Covered

- Phishing attack initiation
- Common phishing indicators
- Email risk classification
- Types of phishing attacks
- Impact of phishing attacks
- Prevention methods
- Security awareness

## Key Takeaways

Phishing remains one of the most common cyber threats affecting both individuals and organisations. User awareness, proper email analysis, and strong security practices are essential in identifying and preventing phishing attacks.
